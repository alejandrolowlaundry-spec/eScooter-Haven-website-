Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2025. This policy explains how eScooterHaven collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Who We Are

eScooterHaven.com ("we," "us," or "our") is an independent electric scooter information and review website operated from the United States. We publish free guides, repair tutorials, and buying advice for electric scooter riders. You can contact us at contact@escooterhaven.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide to us voluntarily, and information collected automatically when you visit our site.

Information you provide:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us through our contact form
  • Name and message — when you submit a contact form inquiry
  • Comments — if you leave a comment on an article (where available)

Information collected automatically:

  • Log data — your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URL
  • Cookies — small files stored on your device (see section 4 below)
  • Analytics data — aggregated information about how visitors use the site, collected via Google Analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our weekly newsletter (only if you subscribed)
  • Respond to your contact form inquiries
  • Understand which content is most helpful to our readers
  • Improve the website experience and identify technical issues
  • Prevent spam and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We will never send you unsolicited commercial email. Every newsletter we send includes an unsubscribe link.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. eScooterHaven uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (e.g., remembering form inputs)
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics to track aggregated, anonymous usage data. This helps us understand which guides are most popular. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.
  • Affiliate cookies — when you click an affiliate link, the retailer may set a cookie to track whether you make a purchase. This does not affect your price.

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the site.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — web fonts loaded from Google's servers
  • Email service provider — used to send newsletters (subscribers only)

We do not use advertising networks that track you across websites for behavioral advertising purposes.

6. Affiliate Disclosure

eScooterHaven participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click a link to a product or service on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.com. We also maintain affiliate relationships with other retailers including REI, Best Buy, and select e-scooter manufacturers.

Our editorial integrity is not for sale. Affiliate commissions do not influence which products we recommend. We only recommend products we have tested or would genuinely recommend to a friend. If we don't like a product, we say so — regardless of whether it has an affiliate program. We clearly disclose affiliate links where required by FTC guidelines.

7. Data Retention

We retain email newsletter subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any newsletter email, and your email will be removed from our list within 48 hours.

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months to allow us to follow up on inquiries and improve our support. After 12 months, messages are deleted.

Analytics data is retained for 26 months as configured in Google Analytics, after which it is automatically deleted.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object — you can object to us processing your data for certain purposes
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@escooterhaven.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

eScooterHaven is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data transmitted to or from our site.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we make significant changes, we will notify newsletter subscribers by email. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@escooterhaven.com
  • Website: escooterhaven.com/contact

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
