About eScooterHaven

We started because riders deserved better. No more vague advice. No more engineer-speak. Just clear, honest guides that actually help.

Our Mission

Honest Guides for Real Riders

We started eScooterHaven because we got tired of vague, generic scooter advice. Every time we searched for help fixing a problem or choosing a scooter, we found articles full of marketing fluff and zero actionable information.

So we decided to build the resource we wished existed. Our mission is simple: give every rider clear, honest, actionable guides — whether you're fixing a flat tire at the side of the road or choosing your very first electric scooter.

We test everything we write about. Our repair guides are built from hours of hands-on experience, not recycled manufacturer manuals. Our buying recommendations are based on real-world riding, not affiliate commissions.

Meet the Team

Three riders who turned their obsession into a resource for the whole community

AR

Founder & Lead Writer

Alex has been riding electric scooters since 2018 and has personally dismantled and repaired over 40 different models. After spending years frustrated by bad online guides, he built eScooterHaven to be the resource he always needed. When he's not writing, he's testing a new scooter on the trails outside Portland, Oregon.

JK

Repair Specialist

Jordan comes from a background in electrical engineering and has worked as an e-scooter technician for a major fleet company. What We Stand For

Three principles that guide everything we publish

✓

Accuracy First

Every guide is researched, tested, and fact-checked before it goes live. If we can't confirm something from personal experience or a verified source, we don't publish it. We'd rather have fewer guides than wrong ones.

🔓

Always Free

We'll never put our guides behind a paywall. Electric scooters are for everyone, and so is the knowledge to fix and enjoy them. We sustain the site through transparent affiliate partnerships — and we only recommend products we genuinely use.

🛴

Real Experience

We don't write from a desk. Our repair guides come from actually breaking down scooters and fixing them. Our buying recommendations come from real riding. This is a site built by riders, for riders — and that makes all the difference.
